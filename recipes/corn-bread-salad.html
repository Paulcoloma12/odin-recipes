Corn Bread Salad

Description

Cornbread salad is a layered dish typically made with crumbled cornbread, seasoned with ranch dressing, and topped with a mix of vegetables like tomatoes, onions, and bell peppers. Often, it includes beans, cheese, and sometimes bacon, creating a savory, hearty salad that's perfect for picnics or potlucks. The flavors blend together to create a delicious, satisfying side dish.

Ingredients

Cornbread:

  • cooking spray
  • 1 (16 ounce) package corn bread mix
  • 1 ½ cups water

Salad:

  • 10 slices bacon
  • 1 ½ cups sour cream
  • 1 ½ cups mayonnaise
  • 1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans pinto beans, drained
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 cup chopped green onion
  • 2 (11 ounce) cans whole kernel corn, drained
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Make the cornbread: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Spray the bottom and sides of an 8x8-inch pan with cooking spray.
  2. Combine cornbread mix and water in a large mixing bowl until smooth; pour into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, about 30 to 35 minutes. Let cool; crumble and set aside.
  4. Make the salad: Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon slices on paper towels.
  5. Whisk sour cream, mayonnaise, and ranch dressing mix together in a medium bowl.
  6. Crumble half of the cornbread in the bottom of a large serving dish. Evenly layer with 1 can of pinto beans, half of the tomatoes, ½ cup green bell pepper, ½ cup green onions, 1 can of corn, 1 cup of cheese, half of the bacon, and half of the sour cream mixture. Repeat the layers.
  7. Cover and chill for at least 2 hours before serving.
